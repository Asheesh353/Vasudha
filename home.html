The Mission

Why We Built NYXARIS

Many children dream of becoming astronauts, but those dreams often fade because space feels too difficult or too far away. Nyxaris makes astronomy simple, interactive, and exciting so anyone can begin exploring the universe. We believe curiosity is where every great journey begins.
